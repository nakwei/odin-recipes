Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Minced Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Water
  • White Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Egg
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water
  3. Season with sugar, parsley, basil, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper
  4. Simmer, covered for about 1 1/2 hours
  5. Boil water and cook lasagna noodles
  6. Mix together ricotta cheese with egg, parsley, and salt
  7. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  8. Spread thin layer meat sauce in bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, followed by the ricotta cheese mixture, then the mozzarella cheese. Continue this layering pattern.
  9. Cover with tinfoil and bake for 25 minutes
  10. Remove tinfoil and continue baking for 25 minutes
