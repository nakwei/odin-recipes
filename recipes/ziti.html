Baked Ziti

Description

This baked ziti is always a hit! A lady I worked with brought this in one day, and everyone loved it. Now it is the favorite of all my dinner guests. I have made this also without the meat, and it is well received.

Ingredients

  • Ziti Pasta
  • Onion
  • Ground Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Provolone Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook ziti pasta.
  2. Brown ground beef and onion in skillet
  3. Stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 15 minutes
  4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  5. Spread ziti onto a baking dish. Top with provolone cheese, sour cream, meat sauce, then with the rest of the ziti, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce. Grate parmesan cheese over the dish.
  6. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes.
